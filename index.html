Horses

Mandalong studs registered its first quarter horse Bar Jez in 1972 a horse we imported through NZ and his descendants are still around today. We now have a small band of mares that have bloodlines that are versatile in both performance and in cow sports. Our aim is to breed a horse that is still working and performing well into latter life.

The stallions that we breed to are selected for different attributes to compliment our mares confirmation and breeding and must have train ability .

Welcome Message

Mandalong studs is located at yarrow station Mendooran NSW it is made up of 5000 or so acres Our mission is to breed quality cattle and quarter horses with sound confirmation and good disposition . With our quarter horses we don't breed quantity but quality. With our cattle we aim to breed the best beef genetics available in this country.

Mandalong studs was established in 1958 by Rick Pisaturo Snr with the aim to produce top quality beef . Today the tradition is carried only Rick and Victoria still with input from the Boss Rick Snr by producing registered Charolais with genetics dating back to the early 1970 's , Mandalong Specials which were noted as a pure breed breed in the mid 1070's and have been exported all over the world and up till today our biggest client being Russia . Tropicanas are a breed we relaunched in 2006 at beef week in Rockhamption with the beef and beer sale at the Cambridge Hotel.

Always trying to improve our genetics we are now producing Mangus and Tangus being a black Mandalong special and black Tropicanas with the aim to satisfy the southern markets .

With all our breeds including Rick Snr new passion race horses , they all wear the HI -5 brand , our own trade mark since the early 90's late 80's and are quite recognizable.
